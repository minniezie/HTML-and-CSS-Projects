Smiling Face with Smiling Eyes

A yellow face with smiling eyes and a broad, closed smile turning up to rosy cheeks. Often expresses genuine happiness and warm, positive feelings.

An emoji form of the ^^ emoticon.

On Snapchat, this emoji next to a contact denotes that you message that person often but they aren't your #1 Best Friend.

 
 
 
 
Smiling Face with Smiling Eyes was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
